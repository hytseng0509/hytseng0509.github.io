About Me

I am a research scientist at Facebook. I recieved my Ph.D. degree from the EECS department at UC Merced in 2021, under the advisement of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. Before coming to UC Merced in 2017, I obtained my B.S. and M.S. degrees from the EE department at National Taiwan University in 2014 and 2016, respectively.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, particularly in content creation, and generative models. I was fortunate to collaborate with great researchers including Lu Jiang (Google), Ce Liu (Google), Weilong Yang (Google), Matt Fisher (Adobe), Cynthia Lu (Adobe), Yijun Li (Adobe), Vova Kim (Adobe), Shalini De Mello (Nvidia), Jonathan Tremblay (Nvidia), Sifei Liu (Nvidia), Stan Birchfield (Nvidia), Jan Kautz (Nvidia), and Jia-Bin Huang (Virginia Tech).
